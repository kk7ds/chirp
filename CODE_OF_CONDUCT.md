# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free
experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, discrimination, hateful language, personal attacks, and other
abusive behavior are not tolerated.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying standards and taking fair,
appropriate corrective action in response to unacceptable behavior.
